Martin's Aquarium

Willy

Willy is a very rare species of fish called the moon snapper.
He's five miles long and was harvested from the fire lake on the moon.
Willy eats rocks.

Moby

Moby is a very rare species of fish called the immortal one.
He's one inch long and was harvested from a puddle in Martin's driveway.
Moby doesn't need to eat as he is immortal.

Flipper

Flipper is a very rare species of fish called the moon snapper predator.
He's five miles and one inch long, making him longer than Willy.
He was harvested from the fire lake on the moon.
Flipper eats moon snappers. Watch out Willy

Bubbles

Bubbles is a very rare species of fish thats just called bubbles because he's the only one.
He changes length often so its impossible to get an accurate size on him,
but he was harvested from Martin's bath tub.
Bubbles doesnt eat but instead drinks water from the tank.

Nemo

Nemo is not very rare at all as he is just a clown fish.
He's 15cm long and was harvested from the Indian Ocean. Nemo eats algae.
